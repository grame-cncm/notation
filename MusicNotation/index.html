Music Notation and Representation
Music notation and representation is part of Grame's research directions. It aims to develop innovative forms of music notation combining symbolic musical notation and performance representations in a real-time and interactive context.

Presentations

  • Introduction to contemporary music notation and representation issues.
  • Guido: a language for music description.
  • INScore: an environment for the design of interactive augmented music scores.
